# Priest Enemy

The priest has a speed aura that can be temporarily removed with the fire tower. If not burning, all non-burning enemies in the area will be healed.

* Heal amount: 8%

* Heal frequency: 3 seconds

* Health: 80%

* Movement speed: 100%

* Damage to player base: 2 HP
