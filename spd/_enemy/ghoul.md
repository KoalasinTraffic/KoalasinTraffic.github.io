# Ghoul Enemy

The ghoul will throw a devastating hook shot that will knock you back if you are in its range. This punch does not affect towers or enemies.

* Punch frequency: 2.5 seconds

* Health: 110%

* Movement speed: 91%

* Damage to player base: 2 HP
